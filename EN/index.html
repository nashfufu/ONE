Welcome to the 'Anonymous' Story Sharing Space.

You are not alone! Sharing your story or facing inner troubles isn't easy. We believe that through sharing and listening, we can find the strength to heal and move forward.

Here, we have some guidelines we hope everyone will follow:

  • 1. Be Respectful: Every shared story deserves sincere respect.
  • 2. Maintain Privacy: Please do not share any discussions outside the group.
  • 3. Take Turns: After listening to 3 people, you get a chance to share.

If you feel you're not ready to share, that's perfectly okay. Sometimes, just listening and feeling that we are not alone is a big comfort.
